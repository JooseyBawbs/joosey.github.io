Create a New Species

Photoshop

Project Description: For this project, I was given various images of different animals, plants and people doing certain actions. In this picture I decided to use the pictures of parrots, butterflies, and a picture of an orange plant. Using Photoshop, I was able to combine the heads of parrots, to the body of a butterfly, with the neck of an orange.
