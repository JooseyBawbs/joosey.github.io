Portrait Animation

Illustrator

Premiere

Project Description: For this project, I had taken a selfie and uploaded that picture to Illustratorthen outlined a basic layout of my face using the pen tool. Then filled in the negative space of the outlined face with multiple swatches that had a variety of colors, such as orange, blue, and purple, and used the eyedropper tool to input the colors. Furthermore, I had taken this outlined image of myself then started editing and animating various shapes, such as, circles, squares and pentagons, and made an animated short video of shapes growing, turning and transforming with my portrait.
