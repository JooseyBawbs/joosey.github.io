Project Description: For this project, I constructed a narrative or story of what occurs in 12 months, which is one year. I used 12 months telling what occurs in each month, this could demonstrate national holidays or important events that occur in each month, for example, in the month of December, we celebrate Christmas, or in June we celebrate the ending of the academic school year and the beginning of Summer.
