Poster Design

Project Description: For this project, I was given the option out of several movie titles to create a custom movie poster for that specific film, the film I chose to make a poster on for was "The Secret Life of Walter Mitty." I had used various pictures, such as Steve Carell walking, landscape of New York City and landscape of Mountain for the background. I wanted the theme to be calm, as I only used different shades of blue, such as light and dark blue.
